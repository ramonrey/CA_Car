Babe Boy

On March 12, 2007 our youngest son, Andre passed away at the age of 16. My best friend Rickey Moore and I decided to search for a 68' Mustang Fastback to restore in Andres's memory. After looking for 9 months, we were successful. Rickey and I traveled to Chanute, KS on December 19,2007 to pick it up and tow it home to California. Rickey passed away on November 22, 2008 before we could begin our own restoration. I saw an ad for The Restomod Shop where I met Pete Moreno, Terry Simpson, as well as Mustangs Plus. What you see before you now is a combination of two memories, Andre and Rickey. - Craig Anderson
